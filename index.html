About BIG

More than investors.
We are operators.

Blackdeer Investment Group is a Midwest-focused, full-service real estate company headquartered in Cottage Grove, Wisconsin. We bring commercial property management, residential management, development, brokerage, and investment expertise under one experienced team.

Our third-party property management division is growing rapidly — because owners trust us to manage their assets with an owner's mindset: protecting value, maximizing income, and communicating clearly every step of the way.
